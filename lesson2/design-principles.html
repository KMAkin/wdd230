White Space and Clean Design

Cassie Lynn's Branding

Caasie Lynn's Website

Cassie's site does a great job of using the white space and clean design principle. She makes sure that the page is not cluttered and is appealing to the eye. She balanced the design between all the elements so that her message could be communicated to the visitors.

Visual Hierarchy

Schultz Schultz_Outfitters

Schultz Outfitters Website

Schultz Outfitters used the visual hieratchy principle. The first thing I notice is them trying to show how amazing it looks when out in the woods or desert. However, their main vocus was around ensuring the vistor knew how to get a hold of someone from their company so they could start planning a hunt using them.

Fitt's Law

Groupon

Groupon's Website

Groupon is using the Fitt's Law principle. They make it so the visitor is not having to hunt the page(s) trying to find what they are looking for. They place big buttons to help narrow the search of the item(s) the visitor is looking for.
