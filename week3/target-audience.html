Queen Creek Arizona Chamber of Commerce

The purpose of the Queen Creek Arizona Chamber of Commerce is to focus on connecting communities with all the local businesses. The site contains many resources to help the local businesses to thrive and be successful. You can see the various activities by looking at the local calendar or search for a particular business.


The site's biggest resource are the people of Queen Creek. It sells the need for people and businesses to join the commerce.


Women in Business Women in Business is a quarterly event that empowers women through mentoring, education and networking. The events feature experienced female speakers who offer support and advice to their female counterparts. class
